About Us

Art for the music you love.

Welcome to ArtInk Studios – where music meets design.

We’re Efe, Rahim, and Sana — a creative team passionate about transforming iconic albums into stunning poster art. At ArtInk Studios, we take already-released albums and reimagine them as bold, artistic posters that fans can proudly display.

Our designs are inspired by the music, the mood, and the message behind each album. Whether it’s a timeless classic or a modern chart-topper, we aim to capture the spirit of the sound and turn it into something visual, collectible, and unforgettable.

Every poster we create is a tribute to the music that moves us — and the artists who make it.

Art for the music you love. That’s what we create.
